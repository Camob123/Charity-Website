Upcoming Events

15 Jun 2023

Summer Festival

Time: 12:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Location: Wythenshawe Park, Manchester

Join us for our annual Summer Festival celebrating Slovak culture with traditional food, music, dance performances, and activities for all ages. Highlights include:

  • Folk dance performances by our community dance group
  • Traditional Slovak food stalls
  • Live music from Slovak bands
  • Children's activities and games
  • Arts and crafts market

22 Jun 2023

Language Workshop: Beginner Slovak

Time: 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM

Location: Community Center, Didsbury

Introduction to Slovak language for beginners. No prior knowledge required. This workshop will cover:

  • Basic greetings and introductions
  • Essential vocabulary for everyday situations
  • Simple conversation practice
  • Introduction to Slovak pronunciation

All materials will be provided. Limited to 15 participants.

Register Add to Calendar
29 Jun 2023

Traditional Cooking Class: Slovak Dumplings

Time: 5:00 PM - 7:30 PM

Location: Community Kitchen, Stockport

Learn how to make traditional Slovak dumplings (bryndzové halušky) in this hands-on cooking class. Our experienced chef will guide you through the process from preparation to serving.

All ingredients and equipment will be provided. Limited to 12 participants.

5 Jul 2023

Children's Folk Dance Workshop

Time: 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Location: Dance Studio, Chorlton

Traditional Slovak folk dance lessons for children ages 5-12. Children will learn basic steps of traditional Slovak dances in a fun and engaging environment.

No prior dance experience required. Comfortable clothing and soft shoes recommended.
