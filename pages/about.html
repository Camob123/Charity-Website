About Our Community

Discover our mission, history, and the vibrant team behind Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum

Why We Do It

For us, this work goes far beyond language. We're here to help people:

Connect with their roots Build identity and confidence Feel part of a welcoming, supportive community Celebrate Slovak traditions in everyday life Whether you're a parent, a young adult, or someone simply wanting to reconnect with Slovak culture—you’re welcome here.

Our Journey

2010

Foundation

Founded by Slovak immigrants with a passion for cultural exchange and community support.

2013

First Cultural Festival

The inaugural Slovak Cultural Festival marked our first big community event.

2015

Educational Programs

We introduced language classes and educational initiatives for all ages.

2018

Charity Status

Officially registered as a charity to enhance our service to the community.

2023

Today

With over 200 active members, our community continues to thrive and evolve.

Community

Education

Inclusion
